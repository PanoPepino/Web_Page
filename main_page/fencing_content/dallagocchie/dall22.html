Giovanni dall'Agocchie (22r.6)

The starting point of each movement is the ending position of the previous one. Link to video explaining sequence.

    First movement

  • Start with your sword in coda lunga stretta (right foot forward).
  • Enemy throws a mandritto to your head.
  • Parry in guardia di faccia, advancing forward right foot, and thrust the point in his face all in one tempo.
  • Return your right foot back. Follow with riverso squalimbro to return to coda lunga alta (left foot forward).

    • Second movement

  • Enemy throws punta imbrocatta at you.
  • Step forward with your right foot, making your left one follow it. Parry with mandritto squalimbro.
  • Follow by riverso tondo to his face all in one tempo. Step left behind right foot.
  • Riverso back into coda lunga stretta.

    • Third movement

  • Enemy responds with mandritto to your leg.
  • Protect yourself with the false edge of your sword.
  • Throw two (man)dritti tramazzone to his head in the same tempo. Last one fall into porta di ferro stretta.

    • Fourth movement

  • Enenmy throws riverso to your upper body.
  • Parry with riverso sgualimbro, passing toward his right side in that tempo with your left foot.
  • Advance immediately with the right one, to wound him with an imbroccata to his face.
  • Follow with dritto tramazzone back to porta di ferro alta.

    • Fifth movement

  • Enemy throws a stoccata to your face.
  • Advance forward with your right foot and go with your sword into guardia d'entrare, joining your true edge onto the enemy's sword all in one tempo, thrusting into his chest.
  • Draw your right foot back throwing a dritto tramazzone, to end in cinghiale porta di ferro.

    • Sixth movement

  • Opponent attacks with a mandritto/riverso/thrust.
  • Hit your true edge into the enemy's sword, and then immediately pass forward with your right foot and stick an imbroccata into his chest followed by a dritto tramazzone to end into porta di ferro larga.

    • Seventh movement

  • Opponent thrusts a punta riversa to your face in order to give you a mandritto tondo.
  • Defend against the thrust with your false edge. When he turns the mandritto, go into guardia di faccia with your sword, sticking your point into his face.
  • Step back with your right foot together with a riverso tramazzone to end in coda lunga alta.

    • Eigth movement

  • Opponent throws a fendente to your head.
  • You pass forward with your left foot (crossing), raising your sword into guardia di testa.
  • You will cast your left hand onto his sword from beneath your own, performing a grip on him
  • Immediately, step forward with your right foot and cut with a mandritto to his head or leg
  • Throw a riverso squalimbro to return into coda lunga stretta.
